Who We Are

Ananta Group was established in 2018 with a clear vision — to support industries at every stage, from concept to final delivery. Since our inception, we have remained committed to delivering reliable, innovative, and cost-effective industrial solutions tailored to meet the evolving needs of our clients.

Our journey commenced under the exemplary leadership of our esteemed founder, Mr. Ambuj Dwivedi , whose profound industry expertise and steadfast dedication have been instrumental in shaping the growth and success of Ananta Group. Through his strategic vision and unwavering commitment, Ananta Group has established itself as a reliable and trusted partner across a diverse array of industries and large-scale infrastructure projects throughout the region.

Ananta Associates

ANANTA Associates, established over 8 years ago, is a trusted provider of automation, electrical, instrumentation, and IT solutions. Based in Satna, Madhya Pradesh, we serve major cement plants, power plants, and refractory industries across India.

With strong engineering expertise, we offer cost-effective, reliable, and innovative solutions that improve industrial efficiency. As an Importer, Exporter, Manufacturer, Trader, and Supplier, we deliver a wide portfolio of multi-brand automation products, backed by quality service and support.

Ananta Minerals

We specialize in the supply of industrial materials, machinery, and industrial minerals, meeting the complex demands of modern industrial operations. Our continued focus on quality, innovation, and timely delivery sets us apart in a competitive market.

Ananta Minerals was established in 2021 with a clear vision to meet the growing industry demand for high-quality industrial minerals, including Bauxite, Laterite, Iron Ore, Imported Gypsum, and more.
